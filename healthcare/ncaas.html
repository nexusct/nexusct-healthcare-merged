Nurse Call as a Service (NCaaS)

Everything you need — equipment, installation, monitoring, maintenance, and support — for one flat monthly fee per bed. Zero CapEx.

How NCaaS Works

Instead of a large capital expenditure, NexusCT spreads the total system cost over a 60-month service agreement with a 20% service premium that covers ongoing support, maintenance, and hardware replacement.

  • No upfront capital investment
  • Equipment, installation & commissioning included
  • 24/7 system monitoring
  • Preventive maintenance & software updates
  • Hardware replacement coverage
  • Priority technical support
